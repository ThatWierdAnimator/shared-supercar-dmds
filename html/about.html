Richard Patterson

Richard Patterson, the CEO & CTO of Trion Supercars, had realized a lifelong dream in May 2012 after 40 years in the automotive industry.

Richard's passion for cars started in his childhood in Detriot. At the age 15 he earned a GM apprenticeship after winning a statewide drafting contest. As a graduate of Mumford High School and Hall of Fame inductee, he had begun his career as a CAD designer at Modern Engineering he quickly rose to high leadership roles because of his high expertise in automotive interior systems.

With over two decades of experience, he brings his visions to life with Trion Supercars, offering high-performance supercars that has the combination of style, speed, and comfort. With the options of ranging from traditional engines to hybrid or even fully electric.
